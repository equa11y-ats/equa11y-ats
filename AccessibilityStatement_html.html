WCAG 2.1 AA Compliant

Our Commitment

At equA11Y aTs, we are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards to achieve WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance.

Accessibility Philosophy

We believe that accessibility is not merely a compliance requirement but a fundamental aspect of creating equitable digital experiences. Our commitment extends beyond our own digital properties to helping our clients create accessible experiences for all users.

Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. Our website is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 level AA. Partially conformant means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard.
